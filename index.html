Crispy Chicken Pizza

Ingredients:

  • Pre-made pizza crust
  • Cooked and shredded chicken
  • Marinara sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Red onion, thinly sliced
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instractions:

  1. Preheat oven according to the pizza crust
  2. Brush the crust with olive oil and spread a layer of marinara sauce.
  3. Sprinkle shredded chicken evenly, followed by mozzarella cheese
  4. Add thinly sliced red onion for a kick. 5. Bake until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  5. Finish with a sprinkle of fresh cilantro.
  6. Slice, serve, and savor the crispiness!

Sri Lankan Hoppers

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups rice flour
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Mix rice flour, coconut milk, yeast, sugar, and let it ferment for 2 hours.
  2. Add baking soda and salt to the batter; mix well.
  3. Heat a hopper pan, pour in a ladle of batter, and swirl to coat the edges.
  4. Cover and cook until the edges are crispy, and the center is set.
  5. Repeat for more hoppers.
  6. Serve these delightful Sri Lankan hoppers with your favorite accompaniments. Enjoy!

Classic Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of your favorite bread
  • 1-2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 3-4 slices of turkey or ham
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato leaves
  • Cheese slices (optional)
  • Olive oil
  • Mustard or your preferred condiment

Instractions:

  1. Spread mayonnaise on one side of each bread slice.
  2. Layer with turkey or ham, lettuce, tomato, and cheese.
  3. Sprinkle shredded chicken evenly, followed by mozzarella cheese
  4. Press the slices together, cut in half, and enjoy your quick and delicious sandwich!
